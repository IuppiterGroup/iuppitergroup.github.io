Portfolio Management · Free

Portfolio Tracker & Stock Screener

Bloomberg-style portfolio analytics on your local machine. Real-time P&L, sector allocation, full financials, and a built-in S&P 500 screener with 12 filter criteria.

Free

Portfolio Tracker runs locally via a lightweight Python web server — open your browser and you have a full institutional-style portfolio dashboard. Track unlimited positions with real-time P&L, cost basis, unrealized gains, sector allocation breakdowns, and interactive performance charts pulled live from Yahoo Finance.

The built-in S&P 500 stock screener lets you filter the entire index by 12 criteria simultaneously — P/E ratio, market cap, dividend yield, beta, profit margins, debt-to-equity, revenue growth, 52-week proximity, and more. Click any result to load full financial statements, income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow data inline. All data is fetched on-demand via yfinance. No accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud.
