About

Independent research and development

Iuppiter Group is an independent research and software firm. We produce original research in quantitative finance and theoretical physics, build professional-grade analytical tools, and develop strategy games rooted in history.

Our work is driven by intellectual curiosity and a commitment to rigor. We don't publish to meet quotas or chase trends — we publish when we have something worth saying.

What We Do

  • Research — Original papers in physics, astrophysics, and quantitative finance. Peer-reviewed quality without the institutional gatekeeping.
  • Financial Tools — DCF models, portfolio trackers, backtesting engines, and filing analyzers. Built for analysts who want precision, not pretty dashboards.
  • Games — Strategy and roguelike games with historical depth. We believe games can be both entertaining and intellectually substantive.
  • Consulting — Available for select research and analytical projects. Get in touch.

Philosophy

We believe in ownership over subscription, substance over polish, and building things that last. Our software runs in your browser or on your machine — we don't hold your data hostage in the cloud. Our research is freely available because knowledge shouldn't have a paywall (tools that save you time do).
