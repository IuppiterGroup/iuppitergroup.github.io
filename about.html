About

Who we are and what drives our work

luppiter Group is a research and consulting firm specializing in finance, science, and strategic analysis.


We publish original research across equity valuation, quantitative trading, applied physics, and public policy. Our software division builds professional-grade analytical tools designed for practitioners who demand rigor and reliability.


Named for the Roman king of the gods — a symbol of authority, order, and far-reaching vision — luppiter Group brings classical discipline to modern analytical challenges.


"Scientia potentia est."
